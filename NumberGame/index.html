The Number Game

Each week, everyone has the chance to guess how many students will be at the weekly meeting. If anyone guesses the correct number, they get 4 points. Without going over, the person with the closest guess gets 3 points, 2nd place gets 2 points, and 3rd place gets 1 point. At the end of each month, we will tally up the points and the person with the most wins a prize!
